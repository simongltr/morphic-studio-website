One line per iteration: what changed and why. Newest at the top.

- **v14** — 2026-06-05 — switch the site header to a transparent bar with the white logo + "MORPHIC STUDIO" name on the left and WORK / ABOUT / SERVICES / CONTACT on the right (Quicksand, wide tracking). Drop the end-of-dolly logo and brand ornaments inside the hero (along with the line-1 translateX shift) — the slogan now stays centered all the way through. - **v13** — 2026-06-04 — make the cinematic transition feel like a real camera dolly: couple now also scales (1 → 4.5×, origin `50% 65%`) so we appear to walk past them, blur ramps earlier (DOF), and they fade out during the pass (0.35–0.7). Background scale increased to 3.0, stage lengthened to 280vh desktop / 220vh mobile for a slower, more cinematic move. Mouse parallax decays twice as fast (`1 - p × 2`) so it disengages before the couple grows. Title and wash timings shifted slightly later to fit the longer arc.
- **v12** — 2026-06-04 — scroll-driven cinematic transition from the dark hero into the light-paper site. A 150vh `.hero-stage` wraps a `position: sticky` hero with four layers (bg / couple / title / wash overlay). Scroll progress drives five CSS vars: bg `scale 1 → 2.6` from the bright center, couple `blur 0 → 16px` + opacity fade, title fades late during the wash, and a `#fafaf8` overlay washes in 0.65–1.0 to land on the v9 paper color. Below the stage, a stub `01 — Le constat` section confirms the seam. Mouse parallax from v11 is preserved and decays with progress. Mobile shortens the stage to 130vh and caps blur at 8px; `prefers-reduced-motion` collapses the cinematic entirely. New helper `screenshot-scroll.ts` captures progress frames 0/25/50/75/100.
- **v11** — 2026-06-04 — add a subtle mouse-driven parallax to the hero: background image stays fixed, while the title (~6/4px max) and couple (~14/8px max) drift toward the cursor. rAF + lerp smoothing, disabled on touch and `prefers-reduced-motion`. Also dropped `mix-blend-mode: multiply` in v10 once we confirmed the couple WebP already has a transparent background.
- **v10** — 2026-06-04 — restart from scratch with a full-page hero only: dark room background image, couple silhouette anchored bottom-center, and the headline "We craft digital / experiences that last" set in Libre Baskerville sitting between the two. Plain HTML + CSS, no Tailwind yet.
