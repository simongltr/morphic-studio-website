# Iteration log

One line per iteration: what changed and why. Newest at the top.

- **v4** — 2026-06-01 — fix the services pinned horizontal scroll asymmetry. v3 had a small left padding but `padding-right: 40vw` on the strip, so the first card entered at the left edge while the last card ended centered — visually unbalanced. Right padding now matches the left (`clamp(20px, 5vw, 64px)`), so the scroll travels edge-to-edge: card 1 sits at the left at start, card 3 sits at the right at end, card 2 centered mid-scroll.
- **v3** — 2026-05-31 — drop the two-column layout. Every section now stacks marker + heading + body full-width, restoring proper visual hierarchy (headings dominate, list items are clearly subordinate). Services section converted to a pinned horizontal scroll: vertical scroll pauses, three cards slide through, then scroll resumes. New helper `screenshot-services-scroll.ts` captures the three card states. Bullet-list typography reduced; pricing/included/FAQ given full content width. Mobile gracefully falls back to a vertical card stack.
- **v2** — 2026-05-31 — type and palette pivot per user feedback. Display swapped from Instrument Serif to **Space Grotesk** (500), body from Hanken Grotesk to **Inter**. Background lifted from warm paper (#f4ede0) to near-white (#fafaf8). Italic emphasis kept but now via sans italic + coral. Roman service/step numerals (i., ii., iii.) replaced with large coral display numerals (01, 02, 03). Display sizes reduced ~15% to accommodate the heavier sans grotesque.
- **v1** — 2026-05-31 — initial design pass. Editorial direction (Instrument Serif + Hanken Grotesk + JetBrains Mono on warm paper #f4ede0, logo colors as sparse accents). Full 12-section site rendered as single-file HTML with embedded CSS. Numbered section markers, asymmetric two-col layout, dark-navy launch block for rhythm. French typography fixed (nbsp before ?/:/!, non-breaking hyphen in "Parlons-en").
